Easy firestorage.jp Uploader



Firestorage.jp supports files up to 2GiB in size.

A POST endpoint has been hardcoded to stay in pure HTML. It most likely works, but you should check for a working endpoint by clicking the following link. You can then pen devtools and change the "action" address to the one provided by the API.
https://firestorage.jp/jsup.cgi

Once the upload is finished, you will receive a URL-encoded response. Paste it into urldecoder.org and decode. The download link is enclosed in quotes and begins with http://firestorage.jp/download/. You can share this link.
